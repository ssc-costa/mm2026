FAQ

The questions people ask right before the move. Usually while staring at a half-packed kitchen.

Can you quote from photos or video?

Yes. WhatsApp photos or a quick walkthrough video usually gives enough detail for an accurate quote.

Do you do Spain–UK moves?

Yes. We run regular routes and help plan the transport timing and practical details.

Do you dismantle and reassemble furniture?

We can help with standard items. Tell us what needs dismantling when you request your quote.

What if access is difficult?

We plan around it. Tight stairwells, long carries, no lift, limited parking, balcony access, and crane options if required.

Do you offer storage short or long term?

Yes. We can collect, store securely, and re-deliver on your schedule.

Do you handle bulky or fragile items?

Yes. Let us know in advance (TVs, glass tables, antiques, safes, large appliances) so we bring the right protection and crew.
